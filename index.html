Theo McKenzie

Hello! I am a Stanford Science Fellow in the Department of Mathematics. 2022-2023, I was an NSF MPS-Ascend Postdoctoral Fellow at Harvard. 2017-2022, I was a Ph.D. student at UC Berkeley, where I was very fortunate to be advised by Nikhil Srivastava and Luca Trevisan. I research the spectral geometry of discrete systems. More generally, I am interested in questions in analysis and probability motivated by statistical physics and computer science. Before my Ph.D., I received my bachelor's degree from Harvard and worked as a trader for 2 years.

In Fall 2026, I will join Yale Statistics and Data Science as an Assistant Professor.

My office is 384-C in Sloan Hall.
